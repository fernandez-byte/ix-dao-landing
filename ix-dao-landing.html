FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are IX Free Points (IX FP)? +

IX FP are earned when you participate in INFAXIA events — not bought, not transferred. They are your access key to Jobs and serve as your participation points as an IX DAO member. The more events you join, the more Jobs you can unlock.

Can I do multiple Jobs? +

Yes — you can complete multiple Jobs as long as you meet the requirements for each. Some Jobs like the UX Job are one-time only. Each Job listing clearly states its own rules.

How long does verification take? +

Verification time varies per Job. Once approved, USDT is credited to your INFAXIA wallet and an email confirmation is sent.

What if my submission gets rejected? +

You will receive an email notification. Low-effort, incomplete, or fraudulent submissions are not eligible for rewards. Admin decisions are final and binding.

How do I withdraw my USDT? +

After admin verification, your reward is credited to the wallet of your choice. You'll receive an email confirmation once payment is processed.

Is this available worldwide? +

Yes — anyone can join as long as you have a verified INFAXIA account and are a member of IX DAO Discord. No location restrictions.
