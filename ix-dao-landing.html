FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are IX Free Points (IX FP)?+

IX Free Points are reward points earned by participating in INFAXIA events and activities. They act as your qualification threshold — once you hold enough, you unlock the ability to take on paid jobs.

Can I do multiple jobs?+

Yes. As long as you meet the requirements for each job, you can complete as many as are available. New jobs are announced regularly in the Discord.

How long does verification take?+

Most submissions are reviewed within 24–48 hours. Once verified, your USDT payout is processed shortly after.

What if my submission gets rejected?+

You'll receive a reason for the rejection. In most cases you can correct the issue and resubmit, as long as it follows the job guidelines.

How do I withdraw my USDT?+

Rewards are credited to the wallet of your choice after verification. You provide your wallet address at payout, and confirmation is sent by email.

Is this available worldwide?+

Yes — IX DAO Jobs are open to verified community members globally, provided you follow the platform standards and eligibility rules.

Need help?+

Head to the IX DAO Discord and open a support ticket. A team member will assist you with any questions about jobs, points, or payouts.
