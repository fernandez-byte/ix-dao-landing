# IX DAO LP — Requirements Definition v1.0
*2026-05-26 — Full version (internal use only)*

---

## Section 0: About IX DAO

**IX DAO is a support organisation of INFAXIA.**

- INFAXIA operates the platform — events, games, marketplace, and user experience
- IX DAO governs the economic infrastructure layer that surrounds and sustains INFAXIA
- Neither replaces the other — both are required for a healthy long-term ecosystem

**IX DAO's 4 support pillars:**

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| Governance | Members vote on ecosystem parameters via IXG — LP conditions, emissions, treasury allocation |
| Treasury Oversight | Manages ecosystem funds; deploys responsibly to support INFAXIA's operations and community programs |
| Liquidity Management | Runs the LP program to deepen IXT liquidity and stabilize the economy INFAXIA runs on |
| Ecosystem Expansion | Funds community initiatives, onboarding programs, and strategic partnerships via DAO proposals |

**Relationship:**
> INFAXIA builds and operates the platform → IX DAO governs the economic infrastructure around it → IX DAO's LP program provides liquidity stability → which feeds back into INFAXIA's sustainability

## Section 1: Definition

IX DAO LP refers to ecosystem participants who provide approved liquidity to designated INFAXIA-related liquidity pools under DAO-approved conditions — contributing to liquidity depth, market stability, trading accessibility, and ecosystem economic continuity.

This is **governance-connected contribution activity** and ecosystem infrastructure support — not a passive income product or financial security.

---

## Section 2: What IX DAO LP IS and IS NOT

**IS ✓**
- Governance-connected contribution activity
- Ecosystem infrastructure support
- Economic stabilization layer
- Long-term utility economy growth

**IS NOT ✗**
- Guaranteed profit participation
- Fixed yield product or APY promise
- Security product or revenue-sharing contract
- Passive income guarantee system

---

## Section 3: Core Principles

1. **Revenue First Alignment** — INFAXIA prioritizes event revenue, participation activity, platform take rate, and marketplace activity before token appreciation assumptions. LP systems support the platform economy, not replace it.
2. **Participation ≠ Guaranteed Reward** — LP participation does not guarantee fixed APY, ROI, token appreciation, or loss protection. No "Play to Earn" structure.
3. **Legal Separation** — IX DAO LP remains structurally separated from direct game outcomes, event rankings, IXP conversion systems, and direct cash reward systems. IXP remains an internal, non-transferable, non-cashable participation unit only.

## Section 6: LP Reward Structure

**Possible Incentives (not guaranteed):**
- **Governance Incentives** — Increased governance weight and voting power
- **DAO Participation Incentives** — Priority access to DAO proposals
- **Ecosystem Contribution Allocation** — DAO-approved treasury allocation
- **Approved Token Emissions** — Dynamically adjustable, subject to governance

**Important Disclosures:**
- Rewards are **not guaranteed**
- Emissions are **dynamically adjustable** by DAO vote
- DAO may **reduce or suspend** incentives at any time
- **Sustainability** overrides short-term attractiveness
- Treasury prioritizes **operational continuity** before yield distribution

---

## Section 7: Risk Disclosures

- **Impermanent Loss Risk** — Asset value relative to holding may decrease due to price divergence
- **Token Volatility Risk** — IXT and other ecosystem tokens may fluctuate significantly
- **Governance Adjustment Risk** — DAO may vote to change LP parameters, emissions, or terms
- **Smart Contract Risk** — Bugs or vulnerabilities may result in loss of funds
- **Liquidity Withdrawal Risk** — Lock periods and unstake cooldowns may limit access
- **No Guaranteed Profitability** — LP participation is ecosystem growth dependency, not a profit instrument

> IX DAO LP must **never** be marketed as "guaranteed passive income," "risk-free yield," "play and earn automatically," or "guaranteed profit pool." Any such representation is prohibited.

---

## Section 8: Governance Control

All parameters — emission rates, lock duration, unstake cooldown, treasury allocation, and supported pools — are controlled through **IXG-connected governance mechanisms**. The DAO manages and operates the LP program, not INFAXIA directly.

1. Support org for existing INFAXIA users (deepening engagement via LP)
2. Growth channel to bring NEW users into INFAXIA

```
Discovers IX DAO (ads/social)
        ↓
Joins IX DAO Discord Server
        ↓
Gets directed to INFAXIA
        ↓
Registers + participates in events → earns IX FP → gets INFAXIA roles

Uses IX FP to unlock Jobs → earns USDT
```

**Entry Point 2 — INFAXIA First:**
```
Discovers INFAXIA (ads/social/events)
        ↓
Registers + participates in events → earns IX FP → gets INFAXIA roles
        ↓

        ↓
```

**Connector:** IX FP + Jobs is the bridge between both ecosystems.

**Primary target (LP program):**

**Not for:**

- Speculators expecting guaranteed APY
