Definition

What is IX DAO LP?

IX DAO LP refers to ecosystem participants who provide approved liquidity to designated INFAXIA-related liquidity pools under DAO-approved conditions — contributing to liquidity depth, market stability, trading accessibility, and ecosystem economic continuity.

This is governance-connected contribution activity and ecosystem infrastructure support — not a passive income product or financial security.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Is IX DAO LP a way to earn passive income? +

No. IX DAO LP is governance-connected ecosystem infrastructure participation. It does not guarantee any fixed return, APY, or passive income. Incentives may exist but are dynamically adjustable by DAO governance and are never guaranteed.

Can I convert IXP to cash through LP? +

No. IXP is prohibited from becoming an external tradable liquidity asset. IXP remains an internal, non-transferable, non-cashable participation unit only. Any IXP → USDT or game-result-based cash-out structure is prohibited by the DAO.

Who controls the LP program parameters? +

IX DAO LP parameters — including emission rates, lock duration, unstake cooldown, treasury allocation, and supported pools — are adjusted through IXG-connected governance mechanisms. The DAO manages and operates the LP program, not INFAXIA directly.

What assets can I provide liquidity with? +

Approved liquidity pools include IXT paired assets, governance-approved stable pairs, and DAO-approved ecosystem assets. Specific pool approvals are subject to DAO governance votes and may change over time.

What happens if the DAO suspends LP incentives? +

The DAO may reduce or suspend LP incentives at any time through governance vote. This is a core design feature, not a bug — ecosystem sustainability and operational continuity take priority over short-term yield attractiveness.
