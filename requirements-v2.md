## Section 0: About IX DAO

**IX DAO is a support organisation of INFAXIA.**

- INFAXIA operates the platform — events, games, marketplace, and user experience
- IX DAO governs the ecosystem layer that surrounds and sustains INFAXIA
- Neither replaces the other — both are required for a healthy long-term ecosystem

**IX DAO** is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization — an organisation with no central boss. Decisions are made by the community through voting, not by a single person or company.

**IX DAO's 4 support pillars:**

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| Governance | Members vote on ecosystem decisions and program parameters |
| Treasury Oversight | Manages ecosystem funds to support INFAXIA's operations and community programs |
| Liquidity Management | Runs the LP program to support ecosystem economic stability |
| Ecosystem Expansion | Funds community initiatives, onboarding programs, and partnerships |

---

## Section 1: Definition

IX DAO LP refers to community members who contribute approved liquidity to designated INFAXIA-related pools under DAO-approved conditions — supporting liquidity depth, market stability, trading accessibility, and ecosystem continuity.

This is **governance-connected community contribution** and ecosystem infrastructure support — not a passive income product or financial security.

---

## Section 2: What IX DAO LP IS and IS NOT

**IS ✓**
- Governance-connected community contribution
- Ecosystem infrastructure support
- Economic stabilization layer
- Long-term ecosystem growth participation

**IS NOT ✗**
- Guaranteed profit participation
- Fixed yield or return promise
- Passive income guarantee system
- A way to earn without contributing

---

## Section 3: Core Principles

1. **Platform First** — INFAXIA prioritizes event revenue, participation activity, and marketplace activity. LP systems support the platform economy, not replace it.
2. **Participation ≠ Guaranteed Reward** — LP participation does not guarantee fixed returns or loss protection.
3. **Legal Separation** — IX DAO LP remains structurally separated from direct game outcomes, event rankings, and direct cash reward systems.

## Section 4: Supported Liquidity Assets

| Approved | Prohibited |
|---|---|
| DAO-approved ecosystem assets | Gameplay-to-cash LP rewards |
| Governance-approved stable pairs | Fixed guaranteed return promises |
| IX DAO approved pool assets | Direct game result payouts |

## Section 5: LP Eligibility Requirements

**Base Requirements (all must be met):**
1. Verified INFAXIA account
2. Active IX DAO membership
3. Staking conditions fulfilled
4. Governance readiness verified
5. Activity and participation history reviewed
6. Community contribution metrics met

**Optional Future Conditions:**
- Rank requirement
- NFT requirement
- Governance participation score
- Anti-abuse verification
- Treasury risk approval

> Conditions are subject to DAO governance adjustment at any time.

---

## Section 6: LP Reward Structure

**Possible Incentives (not guaranteed):**
- **Governance Incentives** — Increased voting weight within IX DAO
- **DAO Participation Incentives** — Priority access to DAO proposals
- **Ecosystem Contribution Allocation** — DAO-approved community allocation
- **Community Rewards** — Dynamically adjustable, subject to DAO governance vote

**Important Disclosures:**
- Rewards are **not guaranteed**
- Incentives are **dynamically adjustable** by DAO vote
- DAO may **reduce or suspend** incentives at any time
- **Sustainability** overrides short-term attractiveness
- Treasury prioritizes **operational continuity** before reward distribution

---

## Section 7: Risk Disclosures

- **Market Risk** — Asset value may decrease due to market conditions
- **Governance Adjustment Risk** — DAO may vote to change LP parameters or terms at any time
- **Liquidity Withdrawal Risk** — Lock periods and cooldowns may limit access to your liquidity
- **No Guaranteed Profitability** — LP participation is ecosystem contribution, not a profit instrument

> IX DAO LP must **never** be marketed as "guaranteed passive income," "risk-free yield," or "guaranteed profit pool." Any such representation is prohibited.

---

## Section 8: Governance Control

All LP parameters — lock duration, cooldown periods, treasury allocation, and supported pools — are controlled through **DAO community governance mechanisms**. The DAO manages and operates the LP program, not INFAXIA directly.
