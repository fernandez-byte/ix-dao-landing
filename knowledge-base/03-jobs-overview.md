# IX DAO Jobs — Overview & Terms

Jobs are structured tasks that allow INFAXIA community members to earn **USDT** upon successful completion and admin verification.

---

## Key Facts

- Rewards are in **USDT** (real money — not IXP)
- Requires **registered and verified INFAXIA account**
- **Admin verification** required before any reward is credited
- Admin decisions are **final and binding**

---

## Job Categories

1. Task-Based Jobs
2. Engagement & Activity Jobs
3. Skill-Based Jobs
4. Social & Community Jobs
5. Event-Related Jobs
6. Economy & Gameplay Jobs

---

## Eligibility Rules

- Member of IX DAO Discord
- Verified INFAXIA account
- IX DAO role obtained
- Sufficient IX Free Points (IX FP)
- One account per person only
- No VPNs, disposable emails, bots, scripts, or automation tools

---

## Reward Rules

- USDT credited only after full completion + admin verification
- Credited to the wallet of your choice
- Email confirmation sent on payout
- INFAXIA may modify, delay, or cancel rewards due to technical issues or violations

---

## Enforcement

- Fake, edited, reused, or manipulated submissions = invalid
- Cheating → disqualification, reward revocation, permanent ban
- Admins monitor submissions, gameplay data, account activity, transaction records

---

## Current Live Jobs

### 1. User Experience Job
- **Reward:** Up to 2.5 USDT
- **Who:** New users only — one-time exclusive
- **Task:** Go through full INFAXIA onboarding and share honest feedback
- **Requires:** Visit LP → register → verify email → create avatar → play a game → submit form with screenshots

### 2. Game Review Job
- **Reward:** 1–2.5 USDT per review, up to 7.5 USDT total (max 3 reviews)
- **Who:** All IX DAO members
- **Eligible Games:** RPS, Treasure Hunt, Car Racing
- **Task:** Play game, submit Game Review Form with authentic screenshots (must include username + timestamp)

### 3. Bug Hunting Job
- **Reward:** 0.5 USDT+ (based on bug severity)
- **Who:** All IX DAO members
- **Scope:** Games, marketplace, wallet, UI
- **Task:** Find and report bugs via Bug Hunting Form
