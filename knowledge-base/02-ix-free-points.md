# IX Free Points (IX FP)

## What It Is

IX Free Points (IX FP) are earned by INFAXIA users for participating in INFAXIA events. They serve as:
1. **Access key** to unlock Jobs in IX DAO
2. **Participation points** as an IX DAO member

---

## Key Facts

- IX FP is **NOT a token** — it is an internal participation reward
- IX FP is **earned through INFAXIA events** — not bought, not transferred
- IX FP is **spent to access Jobs** in IX DAO
- IX FP connects INFAXIA (event participation) → IX DAO (job access) → USDT rewards

---

## Flow

```
User participates in INFAXIA events
            ↓
Earns IX Free Points (IX FP)
            ↓
Uses IX FP to access/unlock Jobs in IX DAO
            ↓
Completes Job → Earns USDT
```

---

## Data Storage

- Stored in **Google Sheet**
- Updated **every 3 hours daily**
- Managed by IX DAO admin team
