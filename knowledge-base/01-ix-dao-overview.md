# IX DAO — Overview & Standard Definitions

## What is IX DAO?

**IX DAO is a support organisation of INFAXIA.**

- INFAXIA operates the platform — events, games, marketplace, and user experience
- IX DAO governs the ecosystem layer that surrounds and sustains INFAXIA
- Neither replaces the other — both are required for a healthy long-term ecosystem

**DAO = Decentralized Autonomous Organization**
An organisation with no central boss. Decisions are made by the community through voting — not by a single person or company.

---

## IX DAO's 4 Support Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| Governance | Members vote on ecosystem decisions and program parameters |
| Treasury Oversight | Manages ecosystem funds to support INFAXIA's operations and community programs |
| Liquidity Management | Runs the LP program to support ecosystem economic stability |
| Ecosystem Expansion | Funds community initiatives, onboarding programs, and partnerships |

## Relationship: IX DAO ↔ INFAXIA

```
INFAXIA builds and operates the platform
        ↓
IX DAO governs the ecosystem infrastructure around it
        ↓
IX DAO's Jobs + LP program support INFAXIA's sustainability
        ↓
Feeds back into INFAXIA's growth
```

## Messaging Rules (Public-Facing)

| Instead of... | Say... |
|---|---|
| "Earn IXT tokens" | "Complete tasks, earn rewards" |
| "DeFi liquidity provider" | "Governance-connected bounty platform" |
| "Crypto yield" | "Participate in ecosystem tasks and get rewarded" |
| "Play to Earn" | "Complete the Job, Get Paid" |

- Use only the DAO definition in public content
- Do NOT use IXP, IXT, IXG, or LP crypto explanations in public-facing content
- Keep all messaging focused on: bounty platform, community, tasks, rewards, and governance
- Token roadmap info → Discord only (not public)
