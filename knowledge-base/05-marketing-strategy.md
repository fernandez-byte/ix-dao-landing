# IX DAO Marketing Strategy

## Positioning

**IX DAO is a bounty/task-reward platform — NOT crypto, NOT Play to Earn.**

| Instead of... | Say... |
|---|---|
| "Earn IXT tokens" | "Complete tasks, earn rewards" |
| "DeFi liquidity provider" | "Governance-connected bounty platform" |
| "Crypto yield" | "Participate in ecosystem tasks and get rewarded" |
| #DeFi #Web3 | #GamingCommunity #Bounty #Tasks #Esports |

**Ad Category:** Gaming / Community Platform / Rewards Program
**No Crypto Ad Certification needed** as long as positioning stays on bounty/task/participation.

## Twitter/X Strategy

### Paid Ads
- Platform: ads.twitter.com
- Ad types: Promoted Tweets, Follower Ads, Awareness Campaign
- Minimum spend: ~$1–5/day
- Target: Gaming, Community, Rewards — not DeFi/Web3

### Organic
- Hashtags: #IXDAO #GamingCommunity #Bounty #Tasks #Esports
- Thread format — higher engagement
- Cross-post to Discord, Telegram, Facebook
- Engage/reply to trending gaming tweets to expose @ix_dao
- Use twitter.py tool once X API credits are loaded

**Recommendation:** Organic first → build traction → then run paid ads

### @ix_dao Account Requirements (before running ads)
- Profile picture + bio (currently empty)
- At least 1 tweet posted
- Verified phone number
- Valid payment method

## Token Roadmap (IXT) — Handling Strategy

**Decision: Token information stays inside Discord only.**

| | X / Public | Discord |
|---|---|---|
| Legal risk | High — public securities concern | Lower — community members only |
| X Ads compliance | Can get flagged | No issue |
| Audience quality | Random | Engaged community members |
| Control | None | Full control |

```
X / Social → Drive traffic → Discord →
  Announce token plans → Community builds →
    Token launch
```

## "Get Paid to Explore" Campaign

**Core message:** You don't have to trust us first. We'll pay you to find out.

### Ad Copy Variant A — Direct
> Play games. Explore the platform. Share your honest feedback.
> Earn up to 2.5 USDT — just for being a new user.
> IX DAO pays you to explore INFAXIA.
> No investment. No risk. One-time exclusive for new members.

### Ad Copy Variant B — Curiosity Hook
> What if you got paid just to try something new?
> IX DAO is looking for first-time explorers of the INFAXIA platform.
> Complete the full experience. Share your honest feedback.
> Earn USDT — straight to your wallet.

### Ad Copy Variant C — Community Angle
> We built something. Now we want to know what you think.
> IX DAO is inviting new users to explore INFAXIA from scratch —
> and rewarding them for their honest experience.
> No investment. No risk. Just explore, play, and get paid.

### Platform Strategy

| Platform | Format | Angle |
|---|---|---|
| Facebook | Boosted post + short video ad | Wide reach, general audience |
| X (@ix_dao) | Tweet thread | Web3 / gaming audience |
| Telegram | Community blast | Existing INFAXIA community |
| Discord | Pinned announcement + bot auto-post | New member onboarding |

### 3-Post Content Series
- **Post 1 — Hook:** "Did you know IX DAO pays you just to explore INFAXIA for the first time?"
- **Post 2 — How:** "Here's exactly how it works: Register → IX DAO role → Explore + play → Submit → Earn USDT"
- **Post 3 — Push:** "This is a one-time job. Once you've done it, it's gone. New users only."
